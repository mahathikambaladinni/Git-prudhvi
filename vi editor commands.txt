Command mode;
gg---Cursor will be at starting position of the file
G---Cursor at ending line of the file
:se nu---to set numbers 
: se nonu---to remove numbers 
:n---to move to certain line where n=1,2,3,
:w--save
:wq---save and exit=:x
:q--quit without saving
/pattern---used to serach fo pattern---press n for next for searching in next lines
shift+$---end of the line
y--copy--p--paste
ny--copy n lines--n=1,2,3,4
dd-delete a line
dw --delete a word
x--delete a letter
ndd---delete n lines  n=1,2,3,4
w--move word by word left to right
b--move word by word right to left
nb or nw---move right to left or left to right n number of words
esc+u---undo
esc+U---undo total line

Insert mode

i--Insert at cursor position
I-Insert at beggining of the line
ESC+ o--Insert in the next new line
Esc + O---Insert in the previous new line
Esc+A---Insert at the end of the line
